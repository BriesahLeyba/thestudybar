Welcome to The Study Bar!

The Study Bar is Longmont's premier workspace cafe designed for students. Whether you need a quiet corner for deep focus or a space to collaborate with classmates, we provide the perfect environment to fuel your productivity.

Why study with us?:

  • Fuel Up: Fresh coffee, sandwiches, and baked snacks.
  • Grow: Weekly tutoring and career workshops.
  • Focus: Private study rooms available for booking.
