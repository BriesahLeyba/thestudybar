About The Study Bar

Located at 123 Street in Longmont, CO, The Study Bar was founded to be more than just a coffee shop. We understand the hustle of student life, which is why we’ve created a modern workspace that helps you recharge while you work.

We believe in community support. Every week, we host events ranging from tutoring sessions to resume workshops. Our mission is to provide the resources students need to succeed, both in and out of the classroom.
